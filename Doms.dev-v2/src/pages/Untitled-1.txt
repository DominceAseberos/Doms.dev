	Overview
	The
	Banana Leaf Detection
	project is a machine learning-powered web application designed to analyze banana leaves and classify their health status. Using advanced image processing techniques and K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) classification, the system can accurately determine whether a banana leaf is:
	Healthy
	: The leaf shows no signs of disease or stress
	Unhealthy
	: The leaf exhibits symptoms of disease or nutrient deficiency
	Not a Leaf
	: The image does not contain a banana leaf
